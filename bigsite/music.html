Music

Music is an art form whose medium is sound organised in time. Common elements include melody, harmony, rhythm, and timbre. Genres range from classical and jazz to rock, hip hop, and electronic music.

Jazz is known for improvisation and swing rhythm, where performers invent melodies on the spot over a chord progression. A melody is a sequence of notes, while harmony is the combination of notes played together.

Many people enjoy a cup of coffee while listening to their favourite music.
