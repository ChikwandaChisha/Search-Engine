Coffee

Coffee is a brewed beverage prepared from roasted coffee beans, the seeds of berries from the Coffea plant. Popular preparations include espresso, cappuccino, latte, and cold brew. The flavour depends on the origin of the beans, the roast level, and the grind size.

Espresso is made by forcing hot water through finely ground beans under high pressure, producing a concentrated shot with a layer of crema on top. Roasting transforms green beans into the aromatic brown beans we grind and brew.

Caffeine, a natural stimulant found in coffee, is one reason the drink is so popular around the world in the morning.
